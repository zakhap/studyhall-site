STUDY HALL

STUDY HALL is a creative building series in Manhattan where all disciplines vibe and play together. At STUDY HALL, everyone’s an expert at something, and no technical experience is required. Presented in partnership with the city’s frontier voices, and hosted by Charlie Freiberg and Zak Hap.
